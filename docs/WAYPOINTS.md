# Prebuilt Waypoints

Lab waypoints included in the HMDMQP-2025 application.

---

## 1. Novel Engineering of Swarm Technologies (NEST)
**Room:** 200C

The lab researches swarm robotics with a focus on coordination algorithms, software engineering, and human–swarm interaction.

---

## 2. Safe Autonomy and Intelligence Lab (SAIL)
**Room:** 200F

The SAIL Lab researches safety-critical control and trustworthy ML to create autonomous systems that are both intelligent and certifiable.

---

## 3. Manipulation and Environmental Robotics Lab (MER)
**Room:** 200D

The MER Lab advances robotic manipulation through vision, control, and machine learning, tackling environmental tasks like recycling and leading benchmarking in dexterous manipulation and active vision.
